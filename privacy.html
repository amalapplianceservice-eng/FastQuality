Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Fast Quality Appliance Repair ("we", "us", "our") operates the website fastquality.us. This page explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit — name, phone number, service address, and the type of appliance you describe when you fill out our "Order Service" form or call us.
  • Technical data — basic log information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, collected automatically by our hosting provider for security and performance.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your service request, schedule appointments, and provide repair services.
  • To contact you about your appointment or follow up on completed work.
  • To maintain the security and performance of our website.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share it only with service providers strictly necessary to operate our business (for example, our email or hosting provider), or when required by law.

Data Retention

We keep service request details only as long as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Your Rights

California residents have the right to know what personal information we hold about them, request its deletion, and opt out of any sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information). To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

Cookies & Third-Party Services

Our website may use basic cookies and third-party resources (such as map tiles from OpenStreetMap and fonts from Google Fonts) that are required for the site to function. These providers may receive standard technical data such as your IP address when the resources load.
