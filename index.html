Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your repair.

Which areas do you serve?

We provide residential and commercial appliance repair throughout Riverside and San Bernardino County, including Riverside, Moreno Valley, Corona, Rialto, Fontana, San Bernardino, Redlands, and the surrounding communities. See our service-area map below.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Yes. Fast Quality is a licensed major appliance repair company (License #50682), and our technicians are experienced and insured, so your home or business is always protected.

How soon can you schedule my repair?

We schedule appointments as quickly as our route allows, typically within a day or two, and we work seven days a week from 9 AM to 6 PM. Call us and we'll find the soonest slot that works for you.

Do you repair commercial appliances for restaurants and businesses?

Absolutely. We service commercial hoods, walk-in freezers and refrigerators, ice machines, fryers, commercial dishwashers, restaurant stoves and ovens, beverage coolers, coin dryers and washers, and more for restaurants, cafés, laundromats, and businesses.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Square. Our technician will provide a clear estimate before any repair work begins.
