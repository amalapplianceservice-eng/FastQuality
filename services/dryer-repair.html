Common problems we fix

Hover over any issue below to see what may be causing it. Our experienced technicians arrive ready to handle the most common dryer repair problems.

  • ✓ Dryer not heatingHover for details

    Usually a blown thermal fuse, a failed heating element, a faulty thermostat, or — for gas dryers — a bad igniter or gas valve coil.

  • ✓ Drum won't spinHover for details

    Often a snapped drive belt, a worn idler pulley, a failed drum roller, or a motor that needs replacement.

  • ✓ Loud thumping or squealingHover for details

    Typically worn drum rollers, a failing idler pulley, or a damaged blower wheel that needs to be replaced.

  • ✓ Clothes take too long to dryHover for details

    Frequently caused by a clogged vent or lint trap, a weak heating element, or restricted airflow that traps moisture in the drum.

  • ✓ Dryer won't startHover for details

    Common causes are a faulty door switch, a broken start switch, a blown thermal fuse, or an issue with the control board.

  • ✓ Dryer shuts off mid-cycleHover for details

    Often overheating from a blocked vent, a failing thermostat, or a motor that cuts out when it gets too hot.
