Common Microwave Problems We Fix

Our experienced technicians arrive ready to handle the most common microwave issues.

01

Microwave not heating

Usually a failed magnetron, a blown high-voltage diode, or a defective capacitor that prevents the microwave from generating heat.

02

Turntable not spinning

Often a broken drive motor, a worn coupler, or a damaged roller guide that keeps the turntable from rotating properly.

03

Sparking inside the microwave

Typically caused by a damaged waveguide cover, exposed metal from peeling interior paint, or a faulty stirrer that misdirects energy.

04

Door won't latch or close

Usually a broken door latch, a worn hinge, or a faulty door switch that prevents the microwave from starting.

05

Microwave runs but doesn't cook evenly

Often a failing magnetron losing power, a malfunctioning stirrer fan, or a turntable issue causing uneven heat distribution.

06

Control panel or buttons not responding

Frequently a faulty membrane switch, a failed control board, or a wiring issue that needs professional diagnosis.
