Common problems we fix

Hover over any issue below to see what may be causing it. Our experienced technicians arrive ready to handle the most common washer repair problems.

  • ✓ Washer won't drainHover for details

    Usually a clogged drain pump or hose, a jammed filter, or a faulty drain pump that needs replacement.

  • ✓ Drum won't spinHover for details

    Often a worn drive belt, a failed motor coupling, a bad lid switch, or a clutch that needs service.

  • ✓ Water leaking during the cycleHover for details

    Typically a worn door boot or gasket, a cracked hose, a loose connection, or a faulty water inlet valve.

  • ✓ Washer won't start or fillHover for details

    Common causes are a faulty lid or door switch, a clogged inlet valve, a tripped control board, or no water supply.

  • ✓ Loud banging or excessive vibrationHover for details

    Frequently worn drum bearings, broken suspension or shock absorbers, or an unbalanced load.

  • ✓ Clothes still wet after the spin cycleHover for details

    Often a drainage issue, a worn belt, or a motor or control fault that weakens the spin.
